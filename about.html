Why do we exist

The number of online scams is booming. Nearly $50 billions was lost globally through online scams. Our mission is to protect consumers worldwide from scams by providing a best-in class tool that prevents online scams and warns you much before its too late.

Meet the team

A group of passionate students that want to put a stop on scammers by creating a tool that is free, easy to use and something that actually works. Making change one tool at a time!
